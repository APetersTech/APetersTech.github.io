The Traffic Jam Story


Built on the site of the Doghouse Saloon in 1965. Michigan's first brewpub. Michigan's smallest licensed dairy. Rooftop garden established in 2011. Innovative cuisine, great beer, wonderful bread and decadent desserts in the heart of Midtown.
